## Features

- **GDPR Compliant**: Meets cookie consent requirements
- **Cookie Policy Link**: Direct link to privacy policy
- **Preference Management**: Link to manage cookie preferences
- **Accept Button**: One-click consent
- **Dismissible**: Can be closed after acceptance
- **Fixed Position**: Stays visible at bottom of screen
- **Responsive**: Works on all devices
